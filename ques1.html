BE - Introduction

BE is the fifth Korean-language (ninth overall) studio album by South Korean boy band BTS. It was released on November 20, 2020, through Big Hit Entertainment (now Hybe Entertainment).

Created in response to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, Be was inspired by BTS' perspective of life during the pandemic. As a sense of helplessness looms in every corner of the world, BTS opens themselves up to express their mixed feelings about this situation—fear and anxiety mingled with the determination to overcome all this.

The record sees the band members contributing to various aspects of album-making process including songwriting, development, production, and visual design.

Music

As usual, BTS didn't focus on the one genre of music, so you'll find alternative hip-hop with acoustic guitar ("Life Goes On"), amazing ballad ("Blue & Grey"),
retro-funk rhythms ("Telepathy"), EDM ("Stay"), and old school hip-hop ("병 (Dis-ease)").

The album is 28 minutes and 30 seconds long and features eight songs.
