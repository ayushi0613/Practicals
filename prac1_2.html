What is a Blog?

A blog is an online journal or informational website displaying information with the latest posts appearing first, at the top.
It is a platform where a writer or a group of writers share their views on an individual subject.

Blogs come in many shapes and sizes these days, with some of them offering videos and photos while others stick to the text-based web logs.
From major brands to small businesses using them, blogs are popular tools to discuss a huge variety of topics.



A Blogger

A blogger is someone who runs and controls a blog.
They share their opinion and knowledge on different topics for a target audience.


Types of Blogs

There can be many types of blogs:

  1. Personal blogs
  2. Collaborative blogs
  3. Corporate/Orgnisational blogs
  4. Microblogging
  5. By genre-
    • Music blogs
    • Political blogs
    • Legal blogs
    • Food blogs
    • Fashion blogs, etc.
